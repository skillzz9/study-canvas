# STUDY CANVAS POMODORO

[Waitlist](studycanvasapp.com)

## Paint art and create shared galleries with your friends while you study!

When you study, sometimes you dont have that much to look forward to and lose motivation, thats why I built Study Canvas. You can chose an image yourself, or let Study Canvas chose an image based on your vibe for the day, and as you set a timer for your study, your avatar will slowly reveal the image. Hold on! If you leave the app and get distracted on your phone during your goal, all your progress will be lost! You can see your paintings in your gallery, which can be shared with study buddies, and you can work on the same paintings if you need help getting through your exam goal! As you rack up time, you will be able to get items for your gallery like candles and windows but also functional items like todo lists, music boxes that play your favorite songs, and post it notes. This makes studying addictive and creative, making you want to upgrade your gallery and make it look as best as possible as a badge of honor.

## How the timer works

1. Create a blank canvas and add it to your gallery. You can set a name, subject, an image or let Study Canvas select your image.
2. Set an overall goal for that painting (10,20 maybe even 50+ hours)
3. Set a time you want to study for (maybe one hour)
4. You start the timer and dont leave the app. If you leave, all that progress will be lost. Invite your friends to help you complete the image by joining the studio!
5. After an hour, part of your painting will be complete! You need to study more to reach your goal!\

## How the Gallery works

1. You can see all your paintings, even the unfinished ones (even though they might bug you, so go study!)
2. You can buy items with the hours of study you put in. Some featured items are a working todo list, a music box that plays music, and post it notes to write on
3. Move around and customize it as much as you like! Its your gallery and your aesthetic!
4. Invite your friends to help with your gallery!
